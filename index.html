Classic or personalised cakes

On top of the mouth-watering mini cakes and tarts, every day you will find a variety of delicious cakes, made with skill and attention according to recipes only known to us. And we are not exaggerating when we say that we have carefully kept the cake recipes as they were made a long long time ago from classic ingredients: eggs, milk, nuts, cocoa, butter, and last but not least, natural, homemade whipped cream. These are our secrets that we choose to share with you today.

And we also want you to know something very important: whatever happy event is going on in your life, with us, you can order a cake exactly as you have always dreamed of. Just contact us and say the magic words: Make it just like grandma used to make it!
